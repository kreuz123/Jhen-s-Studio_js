What We Do

Lush Green Deep Health Concept

The creation of this studio represents the moment my dream came true. Becoming a health coach has always been a passion of mine, and I have worked hard to obtain the necessary certifications. I chose to establish this studio in Taiwan because, after everything had settled down, seeing my mother's slippers reminded me of the importance of home. My philosophy is "living well," which encompasses physical health, freedom in life, and positive relationships. Through this studio, I aim to bring the focus of health back to "people," prioritizing in-person classes. I believe that while online courses offer convenience, they often lack the genuine human connection that in-person interactions provide.
